Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Silni ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Email address - For account creation and authentication
  • Name - For personalization within the app
  • Profile photo - Optional, for your profile display

1.2 Family Member Data

  • Names and relationships - To track your family connections
  • Contact information - When you import from your device contacts
  • Interaction history - Records of calls, visits, and messages you log

1.3 App Usage Data

  • Gamification data - Points, badges, streaks, and achievements
  • Reminder preferences - Your notification settings
  • AI chat conversations - Your interactions with the AI assistant

1.4 Technical Data

  • Device identifiers - For push notifications
  • Crash reports - To improve app stability
  • Performance metrics - To optimize app experience

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and personalize app features
  • Send reminder notifications for family connections
  • Power gamification features (points, badges, streaks)
  • Provide AI-powered relationship advice
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Send important service updates

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

3.1 Supabase

Our primary backend for data storage and authentication. Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers.

3.2 Firebase

  • Cloud Messaging (FCM) - For push notifications
  • Analytics - Anonymous usage statistics

3.3 Sentry

For crash reporting and error tracking to improve app stability.

3.4 DeepSeek AI

Powers our AI assistant feature for relationship advice. Your conversations may be processed by AI services.

3.5 Authentication Providers

  • Google Sign-In - For quick account creation
  • Apple Sign-In - For iOS users

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We use industry-standard security measures
  • Access to user data is strictly limited
  • We do not sell your personal information

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - View all data we have about you
  • Export - Download your data in a portable format
  • Delete - Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out - Disable analytics collection in settings

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion:

  • Personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymous analytics may be retained for improvement
  • Backup data is purged within 90 days

7. Children's Privacy

Silni is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data:
